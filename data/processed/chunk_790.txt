Using this, you can have a fair idea which periods (say months or weeks) you have had the most losses – you can easily adjust the timeframe that you want to work with by adjusting the “Date Range” on top. The date range can be the previous 7 days, 15 days, 30 days, the period since the beginning of the financial year, a previous financial year or any other custom period.
