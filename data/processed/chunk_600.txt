. 3. What is the maximum amount that I can transfer during a day? For UPI, there is no minimum transaction limit, while the maximum amount that you can pay through UPI is ₹ 2 lakh (this also depends on the bank with which you have the account). For Net banking, the minimum amount that you can transfer in a single transaction is ₹50. 4. How many banks do we support in our payment gateway? All the major private and public sector banks are supported by our payment gateways. If you are using net banking for your transactions, then you can use bank accounts from a limited number of banks.
