Equity & Index F&O An equity derivative is a class of financial contracts that invest in equities as the underlying asset (equity stocks/shares in the secondary market). For instance – Reliance Futures is an equity derivative. Its price varies with the movements of the Reliance Share Price. Similarly, for an index-based derivative, the underlying asset is a group of indices such as NIFTY , BANK NIFTY , and FINNIFTY
