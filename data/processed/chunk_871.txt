You can add funds online to your Angel One account by following these steps: Tap on Account at the bottom of your screen. Click on Add Funds . Enter the amount and choose the mode of transfer (Google Pay, PhonePe, other UPI methods, or Net banking). Is it mandatory to add funds via bank account registered with Angel One? Yes, as per regulatory guidelines and compliance norms, it is mandatory to transfer funds through the bank accounts registered with Angel One
